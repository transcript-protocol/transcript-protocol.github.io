ABOUT

EmblemEDU is a cutting-edge application that allows students and alumni to store and share transcripts in a convenient, verifiable, and secure manner. The app offers a fully auditable living record of studentsâ€™ transcript histories where every change has been verified by a trusted third party. Our team is creating a future where cryptography and blockchain technology is used to improve efficiency in the college application process.

There are three user types:

Guidance Counselors

Authenticated as trusted third parties, guidance counselors need only to upload students' transcripts to our platform.

Students & Alumni

Students and alumni receive immediate ownership of their transcripts and can send them to any institution or employer.

Admissions Officers

Admissions officers receive an email with a PDF of the transcript, and can verify its authenticity with the click of a button.

OUR MISSION

The mission of EmblemEDU is to provide students with lifelong ownership of their official academic records, allowing students to store and share these records with any institutions and employers they choose. Our product will alleviate the tedious nature of the college admissions process on both guidance counselors and students, as well as expedite admissions decisions for colleges and universities. EmblemEDU ensures that the records remain secure, verified, and tamper-proof through cryptography and blockchain technology.
